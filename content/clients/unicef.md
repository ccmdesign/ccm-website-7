# UNICEF

UNICEF champions the rights of every child, working in more than 190 countries and territories to protect children from violence, disease, and poverty while ensuring they get the education and healthcare they deserve.

## About UNICEF

UNICEF partners with governments, local organizations, and communities to design responsive solutions that keep children healthy, educated, and safe. From emergency relief to long-term development, the institution connects the global, national, and local work that helps children thrive.
