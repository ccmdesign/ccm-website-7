# Burnes Center for Social Justice

The Burnes Center for Social Justice researches and advocates for practical strategies that strengthen democracy, racial equity, and community-led policy. Its work energizes cross-sector coalitions and shifts in how institutions engage with marginalized populations.

## About the Burnes Center

Their multidisciplinary team blends research, cultural practice, and leadership development to surface actionable insights on justice reform, inclusive governance, and equitable resource distribution across cities and campuses.

## Our Work Together

We collaborated with the Burnes Center to translate dense research narratives into digestible digital experiences, mapping complex frameworks into approachable journeys for civic partners and funders.
