# Meta

Meta builds community across its family of apps, connecting billions of people through social networking, discovery, and shared experiences. Its platform continues to evolve around new formats and technologies while keeping people connected to the causes, businesses, and friends they care about.

## About Meta

Meta invests heavily in research, accessibility, and privacy innovations so the platform can responsibly serve diverse communities around the world. Its engineering and product teams move quickly while keeping meaningful connection front of mind.
