# UC Berkeley

The University of California, Berkeley is a globally recognized public research university that champions academic freedom, innovation, and public service. Berkeley’s scholars and programs span disciplines—fueling discovery and civic impact across the Bay Area and beyond.

## About Berkeley

Berkeley fosters a culture of inquiry that connects rigorous research with real-world challenges. Its labs, centers, and institutes collaborate with governments, nonprofits, and industry partners to advance equity, sustainability, and scientific breakthroughs.
