# Aspen Institute

The Aspen Institute is an international nonprofit that convenes leaders to solve the most critical challenges facing society. Through policy programs, leadership seminars, and public dialogues, the institute fosters thoughtful action grounded in shared values.

## About the Aspen Institute

Its global network of fellows, scholars, and partners spans public policy, the arts, and business. The institute blends convening power with research to influence equitable solutions on education, environment, and economic mobility.
