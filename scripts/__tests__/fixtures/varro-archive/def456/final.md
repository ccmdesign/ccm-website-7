## TL;DR

- Design systems provide consistency across research publications
- Component-based approaches reduce design debt over time
- Research organizations face unique challenges in visual identity

## Introduction

Research organizations operate in a unique space where credibility and clarity are paramount. A well-structured design system ensures that every publication, presentation, and digital asset reinforces the organization's brand and mission.
